About me

From courtrooms to test suites — a non-linear path into tech.

I started my career in Law, but in 2022 I made the leap into IT. I taught myself JavaScript through Argentina Programa and completed a full-stack development program at Coderhouse.

I joined Accenture through a testing bootcamp, and that's where I found my focus. Today I specialize in Playwright + Python and agentic AI — I led the migration from Tosca to Playwright at Banco Patagonia and independently automated a complete banking channel from scratch.

I'm endlessly curious about how large language models and the Model Context Protocol can reshape QA workflows, and I build my own AI-powered tools to put those ideas into practice.
